Discover Negombo

Your gateway to coastal charm and vibrant culture.

Negombo

Negombo Beach

Negombo Beach is a serene escape known for its golden sands and vibrant fishing culture. Enjoy swimming, sunbathing, or exploring the scenic lagoon and lively surroundings. Its close proximity to the airport makes it a perfect start or end point for your journey.

Negombo Market

Immerse yourself in the vibrant atmosphere of Negombo Market, a hub for fresh seafood, exotic produce, and unique local goods. Interact with traders, savor authentic flavors, and discover Sri Lanka’s cultural richness.

Sigiriya

• Cultural Show Sigiriya

The Cultural Show in Sigiriya is a vibrant performance that highlights Sri Lanka’s traditional dance, music, and folklore. Held near the Sigiriya Rock Fortress, this show provides visitors with an immersive experience into the island's cultural heritage. Dancers in colorful attire showcase different regional dance styles, including Kandyan, Low Country, and Sabaragamuwa, often accompanied by energetic drumming and folk instruments. Some performances also incorporate fire dancing, traditional martial arts, and lively rituals. The show is a captivating addition to a visit to Sigiriya, giving a deeper appreciation of Sri Lankan culture and artistry.
